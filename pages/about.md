## ABOUT THE COLLECTION

This digital archive is dedicated to the works and artistic contributions of **Charlie Co, Victorio Edades, and Dante Enage**, three influential figures in Philippine art. Their works span different generations and styles, from modernist expressionism to contemporary social realism and experimental visual art.

From Victorio Edades’ pioneering role in Philippine modernism, to Charlie Co’s bold socio-political imagery, and Dante Enage’s expressive and narrative-driven artworks, this collection highlights their impact on Filipino visual culture and artistic identity.

The collection features:

- A curated selection of major artworks from Charlie Co, Victorio Edades, and Dante Enage  
- Documentation of artistic styles, themes, and influences across different periods  
- Selected exhibitions, publications, and notable contributions to Philippine art history  
- Metadata including artwork titles, creation dates, mediums, and historical context  

This site was created as a digital archive to preserve and showcase the legacy of these artists and their continuing influence on Philippine art and culture.
